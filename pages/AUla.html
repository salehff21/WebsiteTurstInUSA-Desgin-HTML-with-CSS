Al-Ula

The city of AlUla is one of the most important tourist cities in Saudi Arabia, as it is distinguished by the beauty of its picturesque nature and its ancient history dating back to ancient times. Tourism in AlUla is thriving because of its fame for its archaeological monuments and wonderful natural places that attract visitors from all over the world.


  • Elephant Mountain Rock

    It was given this name after a rock resembling the head of an elephant. The area includes several light-colored mountains, and the place is adjacent to cafes, restaurants, and gatherings for women and men. This site is distinguished by its charming natural beauty and its wonderful view of the surrounding area, where visitors can enjoy the stunning views and go on hiking and climbing trips.

  • matal alhura

    Al Hurra View is one of the best tourist sites in AlUla, as it features a wonderful view of the picturesque valleys surrounding it and Elephant Mountain. It is also a great location for camping and enjoying the picturesque nature, and the best time to visit it is before sunrise to watch the sky, or at night to observe the stars with a telescope.
