Jeddah

Jeddah is considered the first tourist place that pilgrims can enjoy, and it is also the main destination for tourists from within the Kingdom, because of its distinctive tourist places, green gardens and historical mosques.


  • Jeddah Corniche

    Jeddah Corniche is one of the most important tourist places in Jeddah, and is considered one of the favorite places for local residents. Because it is one of the largest sea destinations for spending enjoyable times, it is also characterized by constant cleanliness to gain the satisfaction of visitors, in addition to the charming Jeddah Fountain and many international hotels, restaurants and surrounding commercial centers. It is located along the western side of the city of Jeddah and along the Red Sea coast, and can be reached by public transportation. the public.

  • King Fahad's fountain

    King Fahd Fountain is one of the tourist places in Jeddah. It is distinguished by its picturesque shape and towering length, as it is considered one of the tallest fountains in the world. The base of the fountain is in the shape of an incense burner, and the water before it reaches the pump passes through several filters to purify it from dirt and organic materials as it pumps seawater. What makes it even more beautiful is that it is highly illuminated, as it is illuminated by about 500 high-powered floodlights. These floodlights are installed on private islands located on the western coast of Saudi Arabia on the Red Sea in front of the beaches of Jeddah.
