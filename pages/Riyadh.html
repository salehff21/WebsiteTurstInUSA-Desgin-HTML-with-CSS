Riyadh

Riyadh includes many historical landmarks, such as the Masmak Castle, which was built in the middle of the nineteenth century and is one of the most important places. From here, here is a look at some of the tourist places in Riyadh, the best hotels for accommodation, and travel costs.


  • King Abdullah Park

    A wonderful park famous for its tranquil panoramic view. Many visitors come to see its picturesque nature and enjoy seeing the large fountains, parks, and beautiful lakes. You can also enjoy watching the sunset and relax while enjoying the stunning nature. The park overlooks the city of Buraidah, in keeping with the natural topography of the land, as the park is located on King Fahd Road at its intersection with the Eastern Ring Road, so that the park visitor can enjoy an area with a stunning view of the city. Decks have been installed for seating overlooking the city, enabling the park visitor to see a panoramic view. The city from the Plaza Fountain.

  • Snow city

    The Snow City provides a great entertainment atmosphere in a safe snowy environment where the temperature reaches 3 below zero. The area of ​​the city is about 5,000 square meters, allowing it to accommodate 300 people per hour. The Snow City is the first of its kind in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and one of the most famous tourist places in Riyadh and the most visited by tourists
